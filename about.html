About Us

Our Story

Capture Studio was founded in 2010 by award-winning photographer Dr. Rezwanul Alam with a simple mission: to create timeless photographs that tell compelling stories. What began as a small portrait studio has grown into a full-service photography company with a team of talented professionals dedicated to capturing life's most precious moments.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of working with hundreds of clients, from couples celebrating their wedding day to families documenting their growth, and businesses seeking to elevate their brand through powerful imagery. Our approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision, allowing us to create photographs that are both beautiful and meaningful.
